Visit Cameron Park, CA

Enjoy nature and the open skies in this vibrant district close to the state's capital.

Top three activities to do in Cameron Park

Hire a private flight over the city

The local pilots will fly you around for a fun excursion or to your next business meeting.

Take a swim at the local water hole

The lake is divided into two parts one for swimming and one for boating and fishing.

Ride your bike on one of the many beautiful paths

The trees ensure cool riding paths with many stops to enjoy the views.
